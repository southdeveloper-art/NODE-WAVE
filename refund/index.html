Refund Policy

At Node Wave, customer satisfaction is our top priority. Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent.

Cancellation and Refunds

We offer a 24-hour money-back guarantee for initial server setups. If you are not satisfied with your experience, you may request a full refund within 24 hours of purchase.

Exclusions

Refunds will not be issued for service interruptions caused by maintenance, user misconfiguration, or violations of our Terms of Service.

Process

To request a refund, please contact our support team through the Discord server or email us with your order details. Refunds will be processed back to the original payment method.
