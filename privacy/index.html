Privacy Policy

At Node Wave, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

Information Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, purchase a plan, or contact support. This includes your name, email address, phone number, and payment information.

Use of Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, process payments, communicate with you about your account, and enhance the security of our platform.

Data Security

We implement stringent security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. All payment transactions are processed through secure gateway providers.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may share data with trusted partners (like Razorpay for payment processing) who assist us in operating our website and servicing you.
